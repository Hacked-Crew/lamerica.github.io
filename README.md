A satirical and creative manifesto inspired by the USA motto **"In God We Trust"**.

Wordplay, irony, and reflections on contemporary USA culture.

> *"In God We Rust"* — when faith corrodes.
> *"God Less ᵁˢAmerica"* — a country with something missing.
